A long time ago in a galaxy far, far away ....

Episode VII

The Force Awakens

Producer: Kathleen Kennedy J. J. Abrams Bryan Burk George Lucas

Director: Walt Disney Studios J. J. Abrams

Release Date: December 14, 2015

30 years after the defeat of Darth Vader and the Empire, Rey, a scavenger from the planet Jakku

finds a BB-8 droid that knows the whereabouts of the long lost Luke Skywalker.

Rey, as well as a rogue stormtrooper and two smugglers, are thrown into the middle of a battle between the Resistance and the daunting legions of the First Order.

A long time ago in a galaxy far, far away ....

Episode IX

The Rise of Skywalker

Producer: Kathleen Kennedy J. J. Abrams Michelle Rejwan George Lucas

Director J.J. Abrams Walt Disney Studios

Release Date: December 16, 2019

While the First Order continues to ravage the galaxy, Rey finalizes her training as a Jedi. But danger suddenly rises from the ashes as the evil Emperor Palpatine mysteriously returns from the dead.

While working with Finn and Poe Dameron to fulfill a new mission, Rey will not only face Kylo Ren once more

but she will also finally discover the truth about her parents as well as a deadly secret that could determine her future and the fate of the ultimate final showdown that is to come.
